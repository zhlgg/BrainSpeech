Web addiction, often referred to as internet addiction or internet addiction disorder, is a condition characterized by a compulsive or excessive use of the internet that significantly interferes with a person's daily life. This can include spending an excessive amount of time online, prioritizing internet activities over other responsibilities, and experiencing negative consequences as a result of internet use.
